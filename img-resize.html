Image Resizing using seam carving (Image Retargeting)

Seam carving is a technique for image resizing. Unlike rescaling, seam carving keeps the important details of an image and leaves it undistorted. It does this by evaluating the energy of each pixel in the image and from top to bottom, removing the connected pixels with the least calculated energies. The energy of each pixel is calculated by the squared difference between its pixel neighbors.
